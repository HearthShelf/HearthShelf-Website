Introduction

What is HearthShelf?

HearthShelf is a calmer, warmer web client for Audiobookshelf — the self-hosted audiobook and podcast server. It keeps everything that makes Audiobookshelf great and rehouses it in a single, quiet pane of glass: warm dark surfaces, an immersive player, and a cover-glow that tints the room around whatever you're listening to.

It runs as one Docker container alongside your existing server. Nothing to migrate, no second copy of your library — just a nicer way in.

How it works

HearthShelf talks to your Audiobookshelf server over its API. Your library, progress, bookmarks, and users all live on your existing server — HearthShelf simply renders them. Run them side by side; your phone apps keep working exactly as before.

Is it for me?

HearthShelf is for anyone already self-hosting audiobooks who wants the desktop experience to feel less like an admin panel and more like a reading nook. If you value owning your data and a beautiful interface, you're home.
